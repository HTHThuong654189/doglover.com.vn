Welcome to DOG LOVER!

We strongly believe in creating a trusting relationship and unbreakable bond between you and your dog through excellent timing, safe and proven scientific training techniques, consistency, structure, interactive games, positive reinforcement and the understanding that every dog is unique. Our training programs are customized to suit your dogs and your lifestyle. We train dogs of all different breeds and ages. Here at Dog Lover, we specialize in all behavioral issues, obedience training levels, pre-trained family pets, therapy as well as service dog training. We promise to provide you with the best dog training available through our 5-star care and service.

POOL SAFETY

This single 2hr lesson is mandatory for anyone with a pool and a puppy/dog. Did you know every summer 1000’s of pets drown in the family swimming pool. The reason isn’t that they don’t know how to swim, it’s that they don’t know how to get out. Pool classes can be scheduled in home or at Dog Lover.

PUPPY OBEDIENCE

Our loving and qualified puppy trainer understands puppies and is recommended by Veterinarians, Breeders and Other Trainers. We will help you get the behavior patterns you desire. We believe, that a trained and well behaved puppy is a fun puppy and a joy to be around.

BOARD & TRAIN

Your dog will come to live and train with us for 1 week-5 months depending on desired training level. Our qualified trainers will train you dog 3-4 times a day individually and also group train throughout the day in our play groups. Our Board & Train is ideal for busy families that may not have the time to effectively train their dog in home or just need more advanced lessons.

PRIVATE LESSONS

Your dog trainer will come to your home or facility and work hands-on with your dog(s) and everyone involved in your pet’s daily life. A Dog Lover trainer will do a client needs assessment and a behavior evaluation of your dog on the first visit. We will develop short and long-term goals.

SERVICE DOGS

We believe in harnessing a dogs desire to work, their amazing love and devotion, and their intelligence. Training and certifying properly trained service dogs takes a ton of love, time, passion, consistency, ability, and skill. Our team offers those things and more.

ADOPTION

We work with local rescues to find you the perfect pet. Adopting a dog that fits your lifestyle can be harder than it seems. After an initial consultation, the pet professionals at Dog Lover will assist you in locating and adopting a dog that matches the age, activity level and training that you prefer.
